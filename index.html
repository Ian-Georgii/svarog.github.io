Svarog

Pioneering Safe Demining with Aerial Intelligence

The Problem

  • 174,000 km² contaminated
  • 6,000+ casualties worldwide
  • Up to 100 years to clear all mines
  • Ukraine’s farmland: $225M wheat potential at risk

Our Solution

We use a modular sensor platform mounted on drones to detect surface and shallow-depth landmines, combining:

  • Multispectral + Infrared Cameras
  • LiDAR + Ground Penetrating Radar
  • Magnetometers & Electromagnetic Induction
  • AI-powered environmental and terrain analysis

Our Progress

Simulations and real-life testing show reliable mine detection as deep as 5cm in soil. Phase 1 development is complete.

Market Opportunity

  • $100B+ global demining market
  • 5–10× faster clearance using drone-based scanning
  • Potential to reclaim 470,000 hectares of farmland in Ukraine
